My Goals

1. Why do I want to learn this skill?

What does it mean to you? What are your motivations? Will you prove something to yourself or people around you? Will you get a raise because you’re better at your job or will you get a better job?

I want to be able to build an APP that will serve as an online form to gather data for National ID of Philippines and various requirement that affects people's lives and a platform where they can conveniently interact with various services both from government or private businesses.
I am not aiming for a raise but rather a lift for my whole family. I want my children to grow up having a father that owns and runs an ethical business.
I want them to have to visit my office and look up to a future where they will grow and run my company alongside me.

2. What will I achieve if I learn this skill?

Which doors will this skill open for you? Will you be able to earn more, have more flexibility in your work hours, work remotely and travel more?

Learning this skill will give me the understanding on the activity of developers in my company.
I am willing to work more hours just to achieve my dream of building my own tech business.

3. How will this skill change my life and my career?

Would you get a raise at your current job or more respect from your boss once you’ve learnt this skill? Or will you be at Facebook, Amazon or Google instead? How would your life change?

Learning and understanding this skill will change my life as I will have control over what I need to do and be able to actually do it.
I will build my own big company with goals of continuous development of applications that will help businesses and people.

4. How will learning this skill impact the lives of my family, friends and coworkers?

What will you be able to do for your family or friends once you’ve learnt this skill? How will it change the way they think of you? Will your coworkers respect you more?

Learning this skill will enable me to support my family and give them better lives.
They can enjoy life and will be in a much better position to help other people.

5. How will I feel if I never accomplish this?

Would you feel disappointed? Would you feel like you’ve missed out?

I will not fail. I will not waste this opportunity that GOD has given me to learn and acquire new skill that will take me to new boundaries and cross it with much imagination.
Failure will bring shame to my family.

6. What would my life look like if I manage to accomplish this?

Visualise it. What would life look like from the moment you wake up to the time you go to sleep? Will you be living by the sea? Would you kiss your beautiful wife/husband when you wake up?

If I get to accomplish my goal of understanding web development and learning the programming languages that makes applications work as they should, then I consider it a beginning of countless endeavors and projects that will help people beyond their understanding.
